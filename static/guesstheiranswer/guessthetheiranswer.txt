Guess Their Answer is a fun and challenging quiz game where you try to guess the most popular responses given by other players or survey participants. Test your intuition and see how well you can predict common answers!

❓ Objective: Guess the top answers to various questions before time runs out.
🎮 How to Play: Read the question and select the answers you think are most popular.
🧠 Tip: Think about common opinions and popular choices to increase your score!

If you enjoy trivia games and guessing challenges, Guess Their Answer offers entertaining and brain-teasing gameplay!
