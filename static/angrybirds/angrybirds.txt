Angry Birds is a wildly popular physics-based puzzle game where you launch colorful birds with unique abilities to knock down structures and defeat the mischievous pigs. Aim carefully, strategize your shots, and topple all the piggies to win each level!

🐦 Objective: Destroy all the pigs using a limited number of birds.
🎯 How to Play: Pull back the slingshot, adjust angle and power, and release.
💡 Tip: Use each bird’s special skill wisely to maximize damage!

Enjoy addictive gameplay and hundreds of challenging levels in Angry Birds!
