BasketRandom is a casual and unpredictable basketball game where random events keep you on your toes! Shoot hoops, avoid surprises, and aim for the highest score in this fun and dynamic game.

🏀 Objective: Score as many baskets as possible while adapting to random challenges.
🎮 How to Play: Use simple controls to shoot and dodge unexpected obstacles.
⚡ Tip: Stay alert—every shot can change the game!

Enjoy the chaos and excitement with BasketRandom!
