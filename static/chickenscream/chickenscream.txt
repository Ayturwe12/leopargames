Chicken Scream is a unique and fun voice-controlled game where you guide a chicken through challenging obstacles by using your voice volume. Speak louder to make the chicken jump higher and softer to move carefully.

🐔 Objective: Navigate the chicken safely through each level without falling.
🎮 How to Play: Use your voice’s volume to control the chicken’s movements.
🎤 Tip: Practice controlling your voice for precise jumps and timing!

Get ready for a hilarious and interactive experience with Chicken Scream!
