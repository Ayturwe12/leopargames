Google Basketball is a fun and casual basketball shooting game where you try to score as many points as possible by making baskets within a time limit. Perfect for quick, addictive gameplay and improving your shooting skills.

🏀 Objective: Score as many baskets as you can before time runs out.
🎮 How to Play: Use mouse or touch to aim and shoot the basketball into the hoop.
🔥 Tip: Find the right angle and timing for perfect shots to boost your score!

If you enjoy basketball shooting games and quick sports challenges, Google Basketball offers simple yet entertaining gameplay!
