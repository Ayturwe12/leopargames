BoobleShooter is a colorful and addictive bubble shooting game where you aim and fire bubbles to match three or more of the same color. Clear the board before the bubbles reach the bottom and rack up your highest score!

🎯 Objective: Match and pop bubbles to clear levels.
🕹️ How to Play: Aim carefully and shoot bubbles to create combos.
🔥 Tip: Plan your shots to trigger chain reactions for maximum points!

Get ready for endless fun and challenging puzzles in BoobleShooter!
