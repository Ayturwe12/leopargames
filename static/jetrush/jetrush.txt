JetRush is a high-speed arcade racing game where players pilot futuristic jet-powered hovercrafts through neon-lit tracks filled with gravity-defying loops, turbo boosts, and intense aerial maneuvers. Compete against AI or friends in this adrenaline-packed sci-fi racing experience!

How to Play:

Master Controls: Tilt or swipe to navigate through high-speed tracks

Use Boost Wisely: Collect energy to activate temporary speed bursts

Unlock Hovercrafts: Progress through 12 unique futuristic vehicles

Compete in Events: Daily challenges and multiplayer races
