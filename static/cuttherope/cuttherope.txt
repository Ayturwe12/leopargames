Cut the Rope is a delightful physics-based puzzle game where you help a cute little monster named Om Nom get his candy. Cut ropes strategically to feed the candy while collecting stars and avoiding obstacles.

🍬 Objective: Cut ropes to deliver candy to Om Nom.
🎮 How to Play: Use timing and logic to cut ropes in the right order.
🔥 Tip: Plan your cuts to collect all stars and solve puzzles efficiently!

Enjoy charming puzzles and fun challenges in Cut the Rope!
