HungryLamu is a charming and addictive casual game where players help an adorable creature called Lamu munch through colorful fruits and snacks in a vibrant, candy-coated world. Swipe to guide Lamu through tricky obstacles, collect power-ups, and unlock cute costumes in this delightful endless muncher!

How to Play:

Swipe to Move: Guide Lamu left and right to devour floating treats

Avoid Obstacles: Dodge spiky plants and sneaky enemies

Use Power-Ups: Activate shields, magnets, and speed boosts

Unlock Costumes: Earn coins to dress Lamu in fun outfits
