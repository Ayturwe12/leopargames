LearnToFly is a physics-based adventure game where players help a determined penguin achieve his dream of flight. Through trial and error, upgrade your gear, master aerodynamics, and launch yourself into the sky in this charming and addictive progression game.

How to Play:
Build Momentum: Slide down slopes and use ramps to gain speed

Upgrade Equipment: Buy better wings, rockets, and gadgets to fly farther

Complete Challenges: Unlock new stages and bonus objectives

Compete Globally: Aim for the highest distance on leaderboards
