Description:
"Dig Deep" is an addictive resource mining simulator where players excavate precious minerals while managing oxygen, inventory, and underground dangers. With its retro pixel art and endless procedural caves, this indie gem offers the perfect blend of strategy and exploration.

How to Play:

Mine Strategically: Dig through dirt, stone, and rare ore veins

Manage Resources: Balance oxygen tanks and inventory space

Upgrade Gear: Unlock drills, jetpacks, and scanner tech

Avoid Hazards: Lava flows, cave-ins, and mysterious creatures
