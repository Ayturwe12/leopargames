Description:
"Defend Your Castle" is a hilarious physics-based tower defense game where you protect your fortress from invading enemies using creative tactics. With its simple yet addictive gameplay and cartoonish art style, this classic flash-style game offers hours of strategic fun.

How to Play:

Fling Enemies: Click and toss attackers away with your mouse

Build Defenses: Strengthen walls, deploy archers, and upgrade towers

Manage Resources: Collect gold to unlock new defenses

Boss Battles: Face epic giant enemies with special abilities
