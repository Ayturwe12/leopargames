Doodle Jump is a classic endless jumper game where you control a cute character bouncing up platforms while avoiding obstacles and enemies. Aim for the highest score by jumping as high as you can!

🕹️ Objective: Keep jumping upward without falling or hitting enemies.
🎮 How to Play: Tilt or tap to move your character left or right and jump on platforms.
🔥 Tip: Use power-ups and avoid enemies to survive longer!

Get ready for addictive and fun vertical jumping action with Doodle Jump!
