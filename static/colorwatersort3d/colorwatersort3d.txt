Color Water Sort 3D is a relaxing and addictive puzzle game where you sort colorful liquids into matching bottles. Use logic and strategy to pour the liquids correctly and complete each level.

🎨 Objective: Sort all colors so that each bottle contains only one color.
🕹️ How to Play: Tap and pour liquids between bottles without mixing colors.
🔥 Tip: Plan your moves carefully to avoid getting stuck!

Enjoy soothing and challenging puzzles with Color Water Sort 3D!
