DreadHead Parkour is an exciting and fast-paced parkour game where you control a character running through urban environments filled with obstacles. Jump, slide, and dodge to reach the finish line as quickly as possible!

🏃‍♂️ Objective: Navigate through challenging parkour courses without falling or crashing.
🎮 How to Play: Use precise timing to jump, slide, and avoid obstacles.
🔥 Tip: Practice your reflexes and learn the course to improve your time!

Get ready for thrilling parkour action with DreadHead Parkour!
