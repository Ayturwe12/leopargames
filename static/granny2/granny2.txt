Granny 2 is the terrifying sequel where you must escape not just Granny, but her creepy grandson as well. Navigate a larger, more dangerous house filled with new traps and puzzles while avoiding two relentless pursuers.

👵👦 Objective: Find clues, solve puzzles, and escape the house without being caught by Granny or her grandson.
🎮 How to Play: Use WASD or arrow keys to move, interact with objects, and hide to avoid detection.
⚠️ Tip: Be extra cautious—two enemies mean double the danger. Use stealth and quick thinking to survive!

If you loved the first game, Granny 2 delivers even more intense horror survival and stealth challenges!
