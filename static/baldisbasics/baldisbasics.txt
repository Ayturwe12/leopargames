Baldi's Basics is a quirky and creepy indie horror-education game where you navigate a school filled with puzzles and strange characters. Solve math problems to collect notebooks, but beware—Baldi is watching, and getting caught means trouble!

📚 Objective: Collect all notebooks while avoiding Baldi and other odd characters.
🕹️ How to Play: Explore the school, solve challenges, and stay stealthy.
⚠️ Tip: Keep an ear out for Baldi’s footsteps and plan your moves carefully!

Experience a mix of humor and horror in Baldi’s Basics!
