Line Rider is a creative sandbox physics game where players draw tracks for a cartoon sledder to ride on. Combining artistry with physics-based challenges, this unique game lets you design intricate courses, share them online, and watch your sledder triumph (or crash hilariously).

How to Play:
Draw Tracks: Sketch lines, loops, and jumps with the pencil tool

Test Your Design: Watch your sledder navigate your creation in real-time

Add Physics Elements: Use momentum, gravity, and slopes for thrilling rides

Share & Compete: Upload tracks or try community-made challenges
