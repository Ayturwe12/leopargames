Johnny Trigger is a stylish slow-motion action game where players control a badass hitman dodging bullets while taking down enemies with precise shots. With its slick slow-mo mechanics and one-touch controls, this game delivers cinematic gunfights perfect for quick gaming sessions!

How to Play:
Slide & Shoot: Swipe to dodge bullets, tap to fire in slow motion

Chain Kills: Time your shots for stylish multi-kill combos

Unlock Weapons: Upgrade pistols, rifles, and exotic firearms

Beat Bosses: Take on increasingly dangerous enemy factions
