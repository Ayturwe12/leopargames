Backrooms is a thrilling horror exploration game set in an endless maze of monotonous, yellowish rooms. Escape the eerie environment while avoiding unknown entities lurking in the shadows. Can you find your way out before it's too late?

👻 Objective: Navigate the maze and survive the lurking dangers.
🕹️ How to Play: Explore carefully, solve puzzles, and avoid enemies.
⚠️ Tip: Stay alert and trust your instincts to escape the Backrooms!

Prepare for a suspenseful and chilling experience in the Backrooms.
