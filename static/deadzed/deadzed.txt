DeadZed is a thrilling zombie survival shooter game where you fend off waves of undead enemies. Upgrade your weapons, manage resources, and survive as long as possible in this intense action-packed game.

🧟‍♂️ Objective: Defend yourself and survive against endless zombie hordes.
🎮 How to Play: Shoot zombies, upgrade gear, and use tactics to stay alive.
🔥 Tip: Prioritize upgrades and aim for headshots to maximize damage!

Prepare for non-stop zombie action in DeadZed!
