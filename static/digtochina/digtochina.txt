Description:
"Dig to China" is a chaotic physics-based digging game where players tunnel through the Earth's layers with increasingly bizarre tools. From shovels to nuclear drills, outrace molten lava and underground monsters in this wildly addictive simulation.

How to Play:

Upgrade Tools: Start with a spoon, unlock lasers & earthquake machines

Dodge Disasters: Avoid magma pockets, cave monsters, and falling debris

Discover Secrets: Find hidden civilizations and collectible artifacts

Compete: Global leaderboards for fastest China arrival
