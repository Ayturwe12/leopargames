JustFall.lol is a hilarious and chaotic multiplayer battle royale game where players compete to be the last one standing on collapsing platforms. With simple controls and wacky physics, this addictive .io-style game delivers non-stop laughs and intense platforming action!

How to Play:

Jump & Survive: Time your leaps between crumbling platforms

Sabotage Opponents: Knock rivals off edges or trick them into falling

Use Power-ups: Grab temporary shields, speed boosts or knockback hammers

Last Player Standing Wins: Outlast 20+ players in quick 3-minute rounds
