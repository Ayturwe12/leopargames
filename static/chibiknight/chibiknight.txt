Chibi Knight is a charming action-adventure game where you play as a cute knight on a quest to save the kingdom. Explore colorful worlds, battle monsters, and collect treasures while upgrading your skills and weapons.

🛡️ Objective: Defeat enemies and complete quests to protect the realm.
🎮 How to Play: Use sword attacks, dodge enemy strikes, and explore levels.
🔥 Tip: Upgrade your gear and learn enemy patterns to succeed!

Embark on a delightful adventure with Chibi Knight!
