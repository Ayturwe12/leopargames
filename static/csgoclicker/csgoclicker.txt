CSGO Clicker is a casual incremental clicker game inspired by Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. Click to earn points, unlock weapons, and upgrade your arsenal as you progress through exciting challenges!

🎯 Objective: Click to earn points and unlock new weapons and upgrades.
🕹️ How to Play: Tap or click repeatedly to accumulate resources and improve your gear.
🔥 Tip: Upgrade efficiently to maximize your earning rate and unlock rare items faster!

Get ready for addictive clicking fun with CSGO Clicker!
