Five Nights at Baldi’s Redone is a thrilling horror survival game that combines the intense atmosphere of the Five Nights series with the eerie world of Baldi’s Basics. Survive terrifying nights by managing resources, avoiding creepy characters, and solving puzzles to stay alive.

👻 Objective: Survive each night by evading enemies and managing your limited resources.
🎮 How to Play: Use mouse and keyboard to interact with the environment, monitor threats, and stay safe.
⚠️ Tip: Stay calm, keep track of enemy movements, and use your tools wisely to survive!

If you love horror survival games and Five Nights inspired adventures, Five Nights at Baldi’s Redone delivers a spine-chilling experience full of suspense and scares!
