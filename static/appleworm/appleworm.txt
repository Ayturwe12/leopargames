Apple Worm is a fun and addictive arcade game where you control a cute little worm navigating through apples. Eat as many apples as possible while avoiding obstacles and growing longer with each bite!

🍏 Objective: Collect apples and grow your worm without crashing.
🎮 How to Play: Use simple controls to move the worm and dodge dangers.
🔥 Tip: Plan your moves carefully to avoid trapping yourself!

Get ready for a tasty and challenging adventure with Apple Worm!
