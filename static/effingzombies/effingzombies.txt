Effing Zombies is an intense zombie shooter game where you fight off endless waves of the undead using an arsenal of powerful weapons. Survive the apocalypse by upgrading your gear and blasting zombies before they overwhelm you.

🧟‍♂️ Objective: Defend yourself and stay alive against nonstop zombie attacks.
🎮 How to Play: Use WASD to move and mouse to aim and shoot.
💥 Tip: Upgrade your weapons regularly to handle tougher zombie hordes!

If you love fast-paced zombie shooter games, Effing Zombies offers nonstop action and thrilling survival gameplay!
