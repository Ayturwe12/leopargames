BottleJump is a fun and challenging arcade game where you control a bottle that jumps across platforms. Time your jumps perfectly to avoid falling and reach the highest score possible!

🎯 Objective: Jump from platform to platform without falling.
🕹️ How to Play: Tap or click to make the bottle jump with precise timing.
🔥 Tip: Focus and patience are key to mastering each jump!

Get ready for addictive jumping fun with BottleJump!
