Kill the Ice Age Baby Adventure is a satirical action-adventure game where players embark on a comedic quest to stop the infamous Ice Age Baby from causing chaos. Featuring meme-inspired humor, quirky levels, and absurd weapons, this game offers a lighthearted yet challenging experience for players who enjoy internet culture references.

How to Play:
Chase the Baby: Navigate through wacky environments to track down your target

Use Ridiculous Weapons: Fight with everything from frying pans to rocket launchers

Solve Puzzles: Complete meme-themed challenges to progress

Unlock Skins: Customize your character with funny outfits
