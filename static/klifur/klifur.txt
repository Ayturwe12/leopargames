Klifur is an exciting arcade climbing game where players scale a towering mountain while dodging obstacles, collecting power-ups, and racing against time. With simple one-touch controls and endless vertical challenges, this addictive mobile game tests your reflexes and strategy as you aim for the summit!

How to Play:
Tap to Climb: Time your jumps to avoid falling rocks and gaps

Collect Boosters: Grab speed boosts, magnets, and shields

Unlock Characters: Choose different climbers with unique abilities

Compete Globally: Race for the high score on leaderboards
