Drift Boss is an exciting racing game where you master the art of drifting through sharp turns and tricky courses. Control your car’s speed and angle to make smooth drifts and beat challenging levels!

🏎️ Objective: Drift around corners without crashing to complete each track.

🔥 Tip: Practice timing and angle for perfect drifts and higher scores!
