Fish Aquarium is a relaxing simulation game where you create and manage your own underwater paradise. Choose from a variety of colorful fish, decorate your tank, and watch your aquatic friends thrive in a beautifully designed environment.

🐠 Objective: Build and maintain a healthy aquarium by feeding fish and keeping the tank clean.
🎮 How to Play: Use mouse controls to add fish, decorations, and manage tank conditions.
💧 Tip: Balance the tank’s ecosystem to keep your fish happy and healthy!

If you enjoy aquarium simulator games and casual fish care, Fish Aquarium offers a peaceful and engaging underwater experience!
