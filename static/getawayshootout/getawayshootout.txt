Getaway Shootout is a chaotic and fun multiplayer game where players race to escape dangerous situations by jumping, dodging, and shooting their way to the finish line. Compete against friends or AI in fast-paced, hilarious battles!

🏃‍♂️ Objective: Be the first to escape each level by outsmarting and outmaneuvering opponents.
🎮 How to Play: Use arrow keys or WASD to move and jump, and use attacks to slow down rivals.
🔥 Tip: Time your jumps perfectly and use weapons strategically to block your opponents!

If you love multiplayer party games and fast-paced action, Getaway Shootout delivers exciting, laugh-out-loud fun!
