Gobble is a fun and addictive arcade game where you control a hungry character that must gobble up everything in sight while avoiding obstacles. Navigate through colorful levels, collect bonuses, and grow bigger with every bite.

🍽️ Objective: Eat as much as possible without crashing into obstacles.
🎮 How to Play: Use arrow keys or touch controls to move and gobble items.
🔥 Tip: Stay alert and plan your path to maximize your score and survive longer!

If you love arcade eating games and fast-paced challenges, Gobble offers simple yet exciting gameplay for endless fun!
