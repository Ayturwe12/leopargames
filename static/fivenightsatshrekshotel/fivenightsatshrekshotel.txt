Five Nights at Shrek’s Hotel is a spooky and hilarious fan-made horror game where you survive nights in a creepy hotel full of quirky Shrek-themed animatronics. Use your wits and quick reflexes to avoid getting caught and uncover the hotel’s dark secrets.

👹 Objective: Survive the nights by monitoring animatronics and managing limited resources.
🎮 How to Play: Use mouse and keyboard controls to check cameras, close doors, and stay safe.
🕵️‍♂️ Tip: Watch patterns closely and conserve power to last through the night!

If you enjoy Five Nights style horror games with a funny twist, Five Nights at Shrek’s Hotel offers scary fun and unique gameplay!
