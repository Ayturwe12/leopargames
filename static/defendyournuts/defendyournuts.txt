Description:
"Defend Your Nuts" is a wacky tower defense game where you help angry squirrels protect their precious acorns from invading creatures. With its charming cartoon graphics and addictive strategy gameplay, this flash-style classic delivers hilarious animal warfare!

How to Play:

Deploy Squirrel Defenses: Place archers, bombers, and ninja squirrels

Upgrade Units: Spend acorns to boost attack power and range

Survive Waves: 50+ levels with increasing difficulty

Boss Battles: Battle giant raccoons, bulldogs, and more
