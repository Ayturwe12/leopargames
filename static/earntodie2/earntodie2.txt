Earn to Die 2 is the explosive sequel to the hit zombie-crushing driving game. Set in a zombie apocalypse, your mission is to escape across a country overrun by the undead—by any means necessary. Drive, crash, and upgrade your vehicle to blast through endless waves of zombies and debris.

🚗 Objective: Reach the evacuation point by smashing through zombies and obstacles.
🔧 How to Play: Use arrow keys to drive. Earn cash to upgrade your car with better weapons, engines, and armor.
🧟‍♂️ Tip: Don’t spend all your money at once—some upgrades are essential to get past tougher levels!

The apocalypse waits for no one. Start your engine and fight your way to freedom in Earn to Die 2!
