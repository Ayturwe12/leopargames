The iconic Chrome offline dinosaur game where you control a T-Rex sprinting through a desert, jumping over cacti and dodging pterodactyls. This addictive endless runner tests your reflexes with its simple one-button controls and progressively faster speeds.

How to Play:

Press [SPACE] or [UP]: Jump over cacti obstacles

Press [DOWN]: Duck under flying pterodactyls

Survive Longer: Game speeds up every 100 points

Secret Modes: Unlock night mode and rainbow dino
