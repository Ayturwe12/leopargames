Dunk Shot is a fun and addictive basketball shooting game where you aim and shoot hoops to score points. Use your timing and precision to make amazing dunks and beat challenging levels!

🏀 Objective: Score as many baskets as possible before time runs out.
🎮 How to Play: Tap or swipe to aim and shoot the basketball into the hoop.
🔥 Tip: Perfect your timing to score combos and unlock new balls!

Get ready for slam dunk fun with Dunk Shot!
