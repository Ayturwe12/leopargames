Flappy Dunk is a fast-paced arcade game that combines the addictive mechanics of Flappy Bird with basketball slam dunks. Tap to keep your character in the air and score points by dunking through hoops without falling.

🏀 Objective: Keep flying and dunk the ball through as many hoops as possible.
🎮 How to Play: Tap or click to flap and control your height; time your dunks perfectly.
🔥 Tip: Stay steady and focus on rhythm to beat your high score!

If you love arcade games and flappy-style challenges, Flappy Dunk offers endless fun with a sporty twist!
