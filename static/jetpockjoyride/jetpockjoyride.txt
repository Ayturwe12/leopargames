Jetpack Joyride is an endless runner arcade game where players control Barry Steakfries as he zooms through a high-tech lab with a stolen jetpack. Dodge obstacles, collect coins, and complete missions while outrunning the relentless scientists in this action-packed mobile adventure!

How to Play:
Tap to Fly: Simple one-touch controls to ascend and descend

Avoid Hazards: Dodge missiles, lasers, and electric barriers

Upgrade Gear: Unlock new jetpacks, vehicles, and power-ups

Complete Missions: Earn rewards by achieving in-game objectives
