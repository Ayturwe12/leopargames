Apple Shooter is a simple yet thrilling shooting game where your goal is to shoot an apple placed on someone’s head without hitting them! Test your precision and nerves as you carefully aim and time your shots perfectly.

🎯 Objective: Shoot the apple without hurting the person.
🕹️ How to Play: Adjust your aim and fire at the right moment.
⚠️ Tip: Patience and steady hands are key to success!
