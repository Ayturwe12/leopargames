"Doki Doki Literature Club" starts as a cute dating sim/visual novel about writing poetry for anime schoolgirls, but evolves into something far deeper. This genre-defying experience blends psychological horror with meta-narrative twists, delivering one of gaming's most unforgettable stories.

How to Play:

Write Poems: Choose words that appeal to different characters

Build Relationships: Spend time with Sayori, Yuri, Natsuki, or Monika

Expect the Unexpected: Save files may behave strangely...

Content Warning: Themes of depression, self-harm, and existential horror
