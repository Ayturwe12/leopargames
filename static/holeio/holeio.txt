Hole.io is a fun and addictive multiplayer arcade game where players control a black hole that swallows everything in the city! Grow bigger by consuming objects, cars, buildings, and even other players. Battle against rivals in real-time to become the largest hole and dominate the map. Fast-paced, competitive, and full of chaos!

How to Play:

Swallow & Grow: Move your hole to absorb small objects first, then bigger ones as you expand.

Compete Online: Challenge other players in real-time matches.

Avoid Bigger Holes: Don’t get eaten by larger opponents—outmaneuver them!

Dominate the Map: Be the biggest hole before time runs out to win!
