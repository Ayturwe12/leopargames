Kitten Cannon is a hilarious and addictive physics-based game where players launch an adorable kitten from a cannon to see how far it can fly. With simple controls and unpredictable bounces, this quirky game offers endless entertainment as you try to maximize distance while watching the kitten tumble through obstacles!

How to Play:

Aim & Fire: Click to launch the kitten at your desired angle

Bounce & Boost: Hit springs, trampolines and explosives for extra distance

Avoid Hazards: Spikes, saws and other obstacles will stop your flight

Unlock Power-ups: Discover special items that help you fly farther
