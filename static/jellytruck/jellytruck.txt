JellyTruck is a wobbly, physics-based driving game where players control a squishy jelly truck across treacherous terrains. Navigate through challenging obstacle courses with realistic soft-body physics, mastering balance and precision to reach the finish line in this delightfully bouncy adventure!

How to Play:

Balance Your Truck: Tilt carefully to avoid flipping your wobbly vehicle

Conquer Obstacles: Climb steep hills, cross narrow bridges & survive jumps

Collect Stars: Earn points by grabbing collectibles along the way

Unlock New Trucks: Discover special jelly vehicles with unique properties
