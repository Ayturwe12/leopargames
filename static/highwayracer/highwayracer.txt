HighwayRacer is an adrenaline-pumping racing game where players speed through busy highways, dodging traffic, and performing dangerous stunts. Outrun rivals, avoid police chases, and upgrade your car to become the ultimate street racer. With realistic physics and intense gameplay, this is the ultimate test of speed and skill!

How to Play:

Accelerate & Dodge: Swerve through traffic to avoid crashes.

Overtake Rivals: Race against AI opponents to climb the leaderboard.

Escape Cops: Use nitro boosts and sharp turns to lose the police.

Upgrade Cars: Unlock faster vehicles and customize them for better performance.
