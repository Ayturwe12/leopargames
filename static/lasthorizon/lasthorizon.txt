Last Horizon is a captivating space exploration and survival game where players pilot a stranded spacecraft across procedurally generated planets, gathering resources, upgrading their ship, and searching for a new home in a vast, pixel-art universe.

How to Play:
Explore Planets: Land on alien worlds to mine fuel, oxygen, and materials

Manage Survival: Balance resources while avoiding hazards like asteroids and storms

Upgrade Your Ship: Unlock new thrusters, scanners, and hull reinforcements

Find a New Home: Discover the perfect planet to colonize before time runs out
