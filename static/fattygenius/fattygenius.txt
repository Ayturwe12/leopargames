Fatty Genius is a quirky and addictive puzzle game where you help a lovable, chubby character navigate through tricky levels by solving fun challenges. Use your wit and timing to overcome obstacles and reach your goals.

🍔 Objective: Guide Fatty Genius safely through each level by solving puzzles.
🎮 How to Play: Use arrow keys or touch controls to move and interact with the environment.
🧠 Tip: Think creatively and use the environment to your advantage!

Looking for a charming and entertaining puzzle adventure game? Fatty Genius combines humor and brain-teasing challenges for endless fun!
