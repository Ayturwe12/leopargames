IceDodo is a delightful and challenging puzzle-platformer where players guide a cute, flightless dodo bird across treacherous icy landscapes. Slide, jump, and strategize your way through frozen obstacles to help the dodo reach safety in this charming physics-based adventure!

How to Play:

Slide & Glide: Use momentum to navigate slippery ice platforms

Time Your Jumps: Avoid spikes, gaps and melting hazards

Collect Berries: Unlock special dodo costumes

Solve Puzzles: Move ice blocks to create safe paths
