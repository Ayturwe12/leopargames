Drive Mad is a fast-paced car driving game where you race through chaotic city streets, avoiding traffic and obstacles. Use speed and skill to survive and beat your best times!

🏎️ Objective: Drive as far as possible without crashing.
🎮 How to Play: Steer your car to dodge traffic and collect bonuses.
🔥 Tip: Stay focused and use boosts strategically for longer runs!
