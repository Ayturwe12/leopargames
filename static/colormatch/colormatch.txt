ColorMatch is a fun and addictive puzzle game where you match colors to clear the board. Use your strategy and quick thinking to solve colorful challenges and beat levels.

🎨 Objective: Match colors to complete each level.
🕹️ How to Play: Swap or connect colors to form matching groups.
🔥 Tip: Plan your moves ahead to create combos and score higher!

Enjoy vibrant and engaging gameplay with ColorMatch!
