Innkeeper is a charming management simulation game where players take on the role of a medieval inn owner. Build, customize, and run your own fantasy tavern - serve food and drinks to quirky adventurers, manage staff, brew potions, and create the most legendary inn in the kingdom!

How to Play:

Welcome Guests: Serve drinks and meals to diverse fantasy customers

Upgrade Facilities: Expand your inn with new rooms and decorations

Manage Resources: Balance food supplies, ale stocks and gold income

Hire Staff: Recruit bards, cooks and bouncers to help your business

Complete Quests: Fulfill special requests from important patrons
