Age of War 2 is a classic strategy game where you lead your army through the ages — from cavemen to futuristic warriors. Evolve your troops, build defenses, and crush enemy bases as you advance through five different eras of warfare.

💣 Objective: Destroy the enemy base while protecting your own.
⚔️ How to Play: Train units, upgrade your age, and use special attacks wisely.
🧠 Tip: Timing is everything — evolve at the right moment to gain the upper hand!

Can you dominate history and win the war through the ages?
